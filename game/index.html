Zombie Pendemic*

*Pun intended

It's 3.08pm on a grey, misty Tuesday in April. You're in Mr. Mason's Maths lesson, falling asleep to the steady drone of his voice. You're on the verge of sleep, your eyes drifting in and out of focus - when suddenly you notice a zombie, drawn in the corner of your exercise book.

You notice more and more appearing out of nowhere, the onslaught moving towards the turret with every passing frame, and it is your task - nay - your duty, to ensure the turret's survival.

Click on the zombies to shoot them. Different zombies have different values; Red - 1 point, Blue - 2 points, Green - 3 points.

The game gets harder as your score improves, with the mass of zombies ever increasing in number.


Remember - absolutely nobody is counting on you.

This is a figment of your bored imagination.

Good luck.

For you my friend, the lesson is over.
